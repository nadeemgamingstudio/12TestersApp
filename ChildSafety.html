Our Commitment

Protecting children is our highest priority

12 Testers App is a community that connects app developers and testers. We have zero tolerance for any content or activity that involves child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE). We strictly prohibit grooming, sexually explicit material involving minors, or any interaction that endangers children. Our platform is not directed to children under 13, and we take active measures to detect, remove, and report abusive content.

✅ Immediate action: If we discover any CSAE content, we will:
  • Immediately remove the content and ban the responsible user(s).
  • Report to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).
  • Cooperate fully with law enforcement authorities worldwide.

📛 Prohibited behavior & content

The following activities are strictly forbidden on 12 Testers App:

  • Any depiction, discussion, or promotion of child sexual abuse (real, simulated, or illustrated).
  • Grooming, soliciting minors for sexual acts, or sharing contact information to exploit children.
  • Sexually explicit language or imagery involving anyone under 18 years of age.
  • Links to external websites or groups that contain CSAE material.
  • Using the app to arrange real-world meetings with minors for inappropriate purposes.

We use a combination of automated filters (based on keywords and image hashing) and human moderation. All user-generated content – including feedback text, screenshots, profile images, and app listings – is subject to review.

🚨 How to report child safety concerns

Users, parents, or any concerned person can report suspected child exploitation or unsafe interactions through multiple channels:

📱 In-app reporting

Every app listing, user profile, and feedback item includes a “Report” button. Tap it and select “Child safety” as the reason. Reports go directly to our safety team.

✉️ Email reporting

Send details (screenshots, usernames, description) to
nadeemgamer180@gmail.com with subject: “Child Safety Report”.

🔒 Confidentiality & anonymity: You may report anonymously. We will treat every report with the highest urgency and confidentiality. Our goal is to respond within 24 hours for critical issues.

⚖️ Compliance with laws & reporting to authorities

We comply with all applicable child protection laws in every region we operate, including but not limited to:

  • United States: PROTECT Act, 18 U.S.C. § 2256 (CSAM definitions), and mandatory reporting to NCMEC.
  • European Union: GDPR (age of consent 16/13), EU Directive 2011/93/EU on combating child sexual abuse.
  • India: Information Technology Act, 2000 (Section 67B) – Punishment for publishing or transmitting material depicting children in sexually explicit act.
  • UK / Australia / Canada: Local mandatory reporting requirements.

Upon confirmation of any CSAE material, we will immediately:

  1. Preserve all relevant data (logs, user info, timestamps).
  2. File a report with NCMEC’s CyberTipline (or the equivalent local authority).
  3. Provide full cooperation to law enforcement for investigation.

🛡️ Prevention & moderation practices

We implement proactive measures to prevent CSAE before it reaches users:

  • Automated scanning: Image content (profile pictures, uploaded screenshots) is hashed and compared against known CSAM databases (via industry-standard tools).
  • Keyword filtering: In-app chat (if any) and feedback text is filtered for predatory language.
  • Human review team: Trained moderators review flagged content and escalated reports.
  • User education: We display safety tips and remind users to report suspicious behavior.

We also require all developers and testers to agree to our Terms of Use that explicitly prohibit child exploitation. Violation results in permanent ban and possible legal referral.

👪 Age restriction & parental guidance

12 Testers App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has created an account, we will delete the account and all associated data immediately.

Parents or legal guardians who believe their child has accessed our app should contact us at nadeemgamer180@gmail.com. We will assist in removing the child's information and provide guidance.
